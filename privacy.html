Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 21, 2025

Introduction

MemberBot ("we", "us", "our") provides a Discord bot that helps server owners manage member delivery, giveaways, and onboarding. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Discord Identifiers: Server ID, User ID, and role IDs required for bot functionality and administration.
  • Transactional Data: Purchases, coin balances, pack history, timestamps, and transaction IDs for support and refunds.
  • Support Data: Messages and logs you provide when contacting support (optionally stored to help resolve issues).
  • Analytics: Non-identifying usage metrics (e.g. command counts) to improve the service.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate the bot (deliver members, process refunds), provide support, secure accounts, and improve the service. We do not sell personal data.

Third Parties

We may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or payment processing. Those providers have their own privacy policies — please review them before using paid features.

Data Retention

We retain data as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data by contacting support.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting the bot operator via the support server.

Contact

For privacy requests, please join and message us in our support server: https://discord.gg/9CsJUtqWQg
